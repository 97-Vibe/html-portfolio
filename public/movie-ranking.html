Galaxy of the Guardians

One Day

One Day (Thai: แฟนเดย์ แฟนกันแค่วันเดียว,
RTGS: Fanday…Fan Kun Khae Wan Diaw) is a 2016 Thai romance drama film
directed by Banjong Pisanthanakun, and starring Chantavit Dhanasevi and Nittha Jirayungyurn.
In October 2016, Banjong was in Singapore to promote his new romantic drama together with the two leads,
and TV actress Nittha who is making her debut on the big screen.
Super beautiful film. Really artistic.
